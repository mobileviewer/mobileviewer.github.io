How To Use

How to Use Notepad Online

Start writing in seconds — no setup, no sign-in. Here's everything the free online notepad can do for you.

01

Open & Start Typing

Click inside the editor and start typing immediately. Notepad Online is a zero-friction free online notepad — no sign-up, no modal, no waiting. Your first note is ready the moment the page loads.

02

Manage Multiple Notes

Click the + button in the tab bar to open a new note. Each tab stores its own content. Rename any tab by clicking Rename in the status bar. Switch between notes with a single click.

03

Format & Customise

Use the toolbar to choose your font (sans-serif, monospace, or serif), adjust the text size, toggle line numbers, and switch between a dark or light editor background to suit your working style.

04

Find & Replace

Press Ctrl+F to open the find panel. Type your search term, see the match count, navigate with the arrow buttons, and replace one or all occurrences instantly.

05

Preview Markdown

Click the MD toolbar button or press Ctrl+M to toggle a live Markdown preview pane alongside your editor. Headings, links, code blocks, tables and blockquotes all render correctly.

06

Export or Copy

Hit the download buttons to save your note as a .txt, .html, or .md file. Use the copy button to copy all text to your clipboard. Print directly from the browser with the print button.

What is Notepad Online?

Notepad Online is a free, browser-based text editor that works like a supercharged version of the classic Windows Notepad — but accessible from any device, anywhere, with no installation required. It is the fastest way to write notes online without opening a heavy word processor or signing into a cloud account.

Unlike traditional desktop notepads, this online notepad persists your work using your browser's localStorage API. Every note you write is automatically saved as you type and survives page refreshes and browser restarts — until you choose to delete it. Your content never leaves your device.

Whether you're a developer jotting down a code snippet, a student drafting an essay outline, or a professional keeping quick reference notes, Notepad Online gives you a distraction-free environment that's always one tab away.
