What is a Mailto Link?

A mailto link is a special type of hyperlink that opens the visitor's default email client — whether that's Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, or any other — with fields pre-populated. When someone clicks a mailto link on your website, their email program launches instantly with the recipient, subject, and body already filled in.

Our email link generator goes beyond the basics. It supports multiple recipients, CC and BCC fields, priority headers, and four HTML output styles — making it the most complete mailto link creator available for free.
