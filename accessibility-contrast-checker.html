Understanding Contrast Ratios

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) ensure readability by requiring text to have sufficient contrast against its background.

Compliance Levels:

  • Level AA: Ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text.
  • Level AAA: Ratio of 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.
