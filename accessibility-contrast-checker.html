Understanding Contrast Ratios

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are a set of recommendations for making Web content more accessible. To ensure readability, text must have sufficient contrast against its background.

Compliance Levels:

  • Level AA: Requires a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text (18pt or 14pt bold).
  • Level AAA: The highest standard, requiring a ratio of 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text.

How to Use

1. Use the color pickers or enter Hex codes for your text (Foreground) and Background.
2. The tool automatically calculates the ratio based on the relative luminance of each color.
3. Aim for at least "AA" status to ensure your mobile site remains readable in high-glare environments.
