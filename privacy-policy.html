Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Mobile Viewer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website viewer and related tools (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information required to provide and improve the Service.

  • **URL Data:** The website URLs you enter into the viewer are processed on our server to render the preview. We do not store the content or user data from the loaded external websites.
  • **Non-Personal Data:** We automatically collect standard information about your device and usage, such as browser type, operating system, and time spent on pages. This is primarily collected via Google Analytics.
  • **Cookies:** We use cookies for Google Analytics tracking and to maintain your current session state (e.g., your last selected device type).

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the functionality of the Mobile Viewer tools.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized use of the Service.

3. External Websites and Security

When you use our tool to view an external website, we are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or security of those external sites. The viewed content is loaded into an iframe and is subject to the security policies of your web browser.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@mobileviewer.io.
